Bueno 3in1

Project description

3 in 1. Dictionary, exercise book, and multi-language translation app based on google translate api, for general, and language learning purposes.

This app is developed to contribute learning languages in courses, focused on Spanish language based on personal experiencias and claims. This 3 in 1 starter app is edited to to give a starter structure and reusable elements for further development for other languages or general learning purposes. Including firebase based login system, ngCordova camera, inAppBrowser, drawing and printing options.

Multi-language Translation App

Complete Google Translate Api service with 40 languages - Picture upload to translations - Saving and storing translation in localstorage


Dictionary App

Type and find search service for words - Intelligent word profile, distinguishing the verbs and other words - Setting abilities for nouns - Setting abilities for verbs - Picture upload and change opportunity for every word - In-build translation service - InAppBrowser opportunities to help the conjugation of verbs

Exercise Book App

Organized by classes, every class has its own exercise book, with class related text, translation - Including 2 types of exercise book, one to type text by keyboard, other to use pen to write on the screen - Side menu divided translation service and dictionary with the class related words - Printing opportunities for both, the typed, and written notes - Additional camera opportunity
