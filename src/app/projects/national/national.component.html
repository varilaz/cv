Hungarian National Sustainable Strategy

Project description

Between 2005 and 2006 there was organised the development of the Hungarian National Sustainable Strategy, in to the discussion of which the citizens were involeved. To achieve this there was deveolped a website where users could share their opinions about the topics.

The website

The website was divide the 19 main topics, all of which had ow section with the oportunity to send messages, comments, and evaluation by the registrated users. These information was given to site, and was readable by others. The web site was deveolped in HTML, using CSS for decoration, the communication panel, where users could give their opinions was animated by Flash AS2. Th data was stored in a MySql Back end and the communication was organised by PHP.

Responsibiliy

My responsibility was to design the online discussion, develop the suitable website, and maintain the server and the website during the given phase.
